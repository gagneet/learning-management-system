# Tutor Workflow User Guide

Welcome to the AetherLearn Tutor Dashboard! This guide will help you navigate the essential workflows for managing your students, planning sessions, and tracking academic progress.

## 1. Getting Started: The Tutor Dashboard
When you log in, your primary workspace is the **Tutor Dashboard**. It provides:
- **Quick Actions**: Direct links to start a session, plan a session, or record an assessment.
- **My Day Stats**: At-a-glance view of your courses, students, and upcoming sessions.
- **Classes Today**: Your schedule for the current day with direct links to "Go Live".
- **Marking Queue**: Students needing feedback or those with low progress.

## 2. Planning Your Teaching
Effective instruction begins with preparation in the **Session Planner**.

### How to Plan a Session:
1. Navigate to **Planner** from the sidebar or dashboard.
2. Click **Create New Session**.
3. **Step 1 (Details)**: Set the date, time, and session type (Individual/Group).
4. **Step 2 (Students)**: Select the students who will participate. You'll see their recent performance and goals to help you decide.
5. **Step 3 (Content)**: Choose exercises. Use the **Smart Recommendations** (✨) which suggest content based on your students' average performance.
6. **Step 4 (Structure)**: (Optional) Break your session into blocks like "Warm-up", "Main Activity", and "Wrap-up".
7. **Step 5 (Resources)**: Note down any physical materials (like worksheets or maps) you need to prepare.
8. **Step 6 (Objectives)**: Define what you want to achieve and set contingency plans.

## 3. Managing Students
The **Students** section is where you manage individual learning paths.

### Viewing Student Profiles:
- Click **View Full Profile** on any student in your list.
- **Overview**: See their academic profile (Reading Age, Numeracy Age) and current course progress.
- **Strengths & Weaknesses**: View and add observations about the student's learning style and key areas for improvement.
- **Notes**: Add internal notes or parent-visible comments.
- **Activity**: Review their full history of sessions, homework, and attendance.

## 4. Academic Assessments
Regular assessments are key to tracking level advancement.

### Recording an Assessment:
1. Go to **Assessments → New Assessment**.
2. Select the student and the subject course.
3. Update their **Assessed Grade Level** and specific ages (Reading/Numeracy).
4. Add detailed observations in the **Notes** field.
5. Submitting this will automatically update the student's profile and create a historical log.

## 5. Content Library
Explore the **Content Library** to find materials for your sessions.
- Browse by **Courses** to see structured content units.
- Switch to **All Exercises** to search for specific activities by type or difficulty.
- You can **Preview** exercises before assigning them.

## 6. Real-time Session Interaction
During a live session:
- Monitor student progress in real-time.
- Respond to **Help Requests** immediately.
- Use the built-in video conferencing (if enabled) for face-to-face instruction.
- Record session notes that sync directly to the student's profile.

---
*Tip: Always check your **Marking Queue** at the start of your shift to ensure no student is falling behind!*
